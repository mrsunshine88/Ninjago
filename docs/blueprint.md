# **App Name**: Ninjago: Elemental Clash

## Core Features:

- Player Identity Input: Players can enter a unique name that will be displayed throughout the game and on the leaderboard.
- Ninja Character Selection: Allows players to choose one of six Ninjago heroes, each associated with a distinct elemental power, which influences AI-driven encounter outcomes.
- Dynamic Encounter Progression: A structured sequence of levels, introducing various enemies and mini-bosses up to the final boss, tracking player progress.
- AI-Powered Encounter Outcome Tool: Utilizes generative AI to dynamically describe the outcome of battles or interactions based on the chosen Ninja's elemental power against the specific enemy encountered.
- Score and Achievement Display: Presents the player's final score upon game completion and displays a 'HIGH SCORE' notification if a new record is achieved.
- Global Leaderboard Management: Stores and displays the top 15 player scores and names, dynamically updating after each game session.
- Game Reset and Menu Navigation: Automatically returns the player to the start screen after a game, allowing for name changes, ninja re-selection, and a new game start, with the updated leaderboard visible.

## Style Guidelines:

- The chosen color scheme is dark, reflecting the adventurous and often serious tone of the Ninjago world, enhancing the vibrancy of character-specific elements.
- Primary color: A bold, heroic red (#F71F47), drawing inspiration from Kai's elemental fire power and symbolizing action and energy. (HSL: 345, 90%, 65%)
- Background color: A deep, muted charcoal-red (#2F2325), providing a subtle texture that aligns with the primary color while maintaining a focused visual environment. (HSL: 345, 20%, 15%)
- Accent color: A striking magenta-purple (#EC85DB), analogous to the primary and evoking mystical energy and contrast against the dark background. (HSL: 315, 70%, 75%)
- All text will use the 'Space Grotesk' font, a proportional sans-serif. Its clean, techy, and modern aesthetic aligns with the dynamic nature of a ninja-themed adventure game, suitable for both headlines and concise body text.
- Implement stylized icons for each elemental power and Ninja character, using clean lines and a modern geometric approach to maintain visual consistency with the UI.
- Focus on a clear, high-contrast layout, utilizing defined sections for game elements like player stats, enemy encounters, and the leaderboard, ensuring readability and immersive experience on a dark theme.
